Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Startalk Daily Scoop ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application Automation Posting, which integrates with Meta technologies to publish and manage content on our official Facebook Page (the “Service”).

Scope

This policy applies only to the information processed by our Service for managing our Facebook Page. It does not cover Facebook’s own practices. Please also review Meta’s Terms and Data Policy.

Information We Process

  • Facebook Page data: Page ID, Page name, posts, comments, reactions, and basic engagement metrics necessary to manage Page content.
  • Access tokens: Page or user tokens issued by Meta to authenticate our app. These are used only to perform actions you authorize (e.g., publish a post) and are never shared publicly.
  • Operational logs (limited): Timestamps and IDs of published posts or API calls for troubleshooting and audit purposes.

We do not collect personal information from end users who view the Page. We do not collect sensitive categories of data.

Facebook Permissions We Request

  • pages_manage_posts – publish and manage Page content.
  • pages_read_engagement – read engagement and insights to monitor performance.
  • pages_show_list – identify Pages you manage to let you choose a Page.
  • pages_manage_engagement (optional) – manage comments and replies.

How We Use Information

  • To schedule, publish, edit, and delete posts on the Facebook Page.
  • To reply to comments and moderate Page interactions (if enabled).
  • To measure post performance and improve our content strategy.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the Service.

Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

  • Legitimate interests: operating and improving our Page communications.
  • Contract/consent: where applicable for admins using the Service.

Data Storage & Security

  • Access tokens are stored securely (e.g., encrypted at rest, limited access).
  • We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Retention

We retain operational logs and tokens only for as long as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Tokens are removed when revoked or when no longer required.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited data with service providers that help us host, operate, or monitor the Service under confidentiality and data protection obligations, or when required by law.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Admins can revoke our app’s Page permissions at any time in Facebook/Accounts Center settings.
  • Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete information held by us. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Children’s Privacy

Our Service is intended for use by Page admins and is not directed to children.

International Transfers

If we transfer information outside your region, we do so in accordance with applicable law and with appropriate safeguards.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a new “Last updated” date.
